Introduction

Our application based on the smartphone is design for customers using the autonomous shopping cart on the supermarket, which helps the users fast and easy to find the specific items. When people are shopping, the shopping cart will automatically follow them. What’s more, users can automatically checkout through our app, no need to queue or manual checkout, saving time and effort. The shopping cart will automatically scan and weigh when the item is placed.

Our Distinct Functions

QR-Code: Users can use Bluetooth to connect to mobile phones and autonomous shopping carts via QR code.

Shopping list: users could enter the shopping list whenever they want.

AR navigation: users could use the AR navigation to find the products they want. After “entered the shopping list” or “search items,” users could press the “navigation” button to start navigating.
